Bamboozle
Mirror Image
Track this cooldown!!! You MUST have this up when you start your cycle. Slaying a clone give you Damage Bonus.
Satsujin
This will consume ALL Marks from the enemy. This has a PER ENEMY cooldown, keep track of this too.
Surprise Strike
Dealing damage while in Vanish will increase the damage of your hit.
Marked
Spin-Attack will be used to apply this. This is a huge damage increase.
Backstab
This replaces MultiHit but doesn't replace Bamboozle. You must face the same direction as the enemy while cycling if you have this.
Sandbagging
This is very important. When you are doing your cycle you must be in the enemy to reduce the cooldown of Mirror Image.
Mirage
Summons a clone after using Bamboozle or Teleporting to your Shadow Projection. This clone can activate Sandbagging.
Foul Play
This will make Bamboozle kill a Mirror Clone instead of having a cooldown.
Last Laugh
This will make Mirror Clones cast MultiHit or Backstab when killed. Other clones will cast any one of your spells.
