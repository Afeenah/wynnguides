Awakened
You want to build up Awakened by spamming Totem when you have Mask of the Fanatic (Using high +Totem Cost item like Lower). During Awakened, you also want to have Mask of the Fanatic on to activate Totemic Shatter.
Mystic Masks
Most of the time you would want to be on Awakened to activate Totemic Shatter.
Tribal Chants
This node gives significant buffs to yourself and your teammates, make sure to refresh these buffs on cooldown.
Exploding Puppets
This interacts with the Strings of Fate Major Id, which triggers this node instantly when the Puppets spawn.
Totemic Shatter
Toteminstantly activates all it's effects, including summoning Puppets , Healing, and Damage. Must have Awakened.
Haunting Memory
Throws your previous mask when switching giving the enemy a debuff, very good support for teammates.
