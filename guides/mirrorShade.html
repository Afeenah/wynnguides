Bamboozle
Mirror Image
Track this cooldown!!! You MUST have this up when you start your cycle. Slaying a clone give you Damage Bonus.
Satsujin
This will consume ALL Marked from the enemy. This has a PER ENEMY cooldown, keep track of this too.
Surprise Strike
Dealing damage while in Vanished will increase the damage of your hit.
Marked
Spin-Attack will be used to apply this. This is a huge damage increase.
Backstab
This replaces Multi-Hit but doesn't replace Bamboozle. You must face the same direction as the enemy while cycling if you have this.
Sand-bagging
This is very important. When you are doing your cycle you must be in the enemy to reduce the cooldown of Mirror Image.
Mirage
Summons a clone after using Bamboozle or Teleporting to your Shadow Projection This clone can activate Sandbagging.
Foul Play
This will make Bamboozle kill a Mirror Clone instead of having a cooldown.
Last Laugh
This will make Mirror Clone cast Multi-Hit or Backstab when killed. Other clones will cast any one of your spells.
