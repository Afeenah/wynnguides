Powder Special
This replaces Backstab as your heavy hit. You want to Melee to build the Powder Special bar. Once it is fully charged, you can [SHIFT] + Melee to cast it, dealing massive damage.
Marked
Spin-Attack and Smoke-Bomb will be used to apply this. This is a huge damage increase.
Surprise Strike
When in Vanish, on your next hit, you will be revealed and deal +80% more damage.
Satsujin
This will consume ALL Marks from the enemy to deal even more damage. This has a PER ENEMY cooldown, keep track of this.
Mirror Image
Slaying a clone give you +30% Damage Bonus for 15s. You can force it by casting Bamboozle!
Nightcloak Knives
You can get these by casting Spin-Attack while in Vanish when the enemy has 2 or more Marks. These Knives will attack with you dealing more damage.
Silent Killer
This resets your Vanish cooldown entirely after killing an enemy. You want to stay in Vanish when playing H-Melee Shadestepper.
