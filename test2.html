Swan Dive
Build Momentum, the more you have the more damage you deal when you fall. If you don't have Righting Reflex press [SHIFT] to plunge. Dancing Blade launches you up while having +3 Momentum. When you fall Braced Impact gives 50% Resistance, Petal Storm increases damage dealt when having max Jasmine Bloom.
Smoke Bomb
Cast this every 3 cycles or to reposition.
Shurikens
If you build has this then after Multihit to fire.
Aerial Ace
Hitting an enemy with Swan Dive will get you +35% Damage Boost for 7.5s you want to try to maintain this.
Weightless
+1 when 1.25+ blocks in the air. This is your mana printing ability.
Serpent's Garden
This is Acrobat's ultimate ability, in many cases more than Doubles your DPS. Use wisely as enemies can move away from your Garden!!! (This problem can be negated with the Efflorescence Major Id).
-- Aspects --
Acrobat's Embodiment of Gravity Defiance
Overall Good aspect, Gives extra Lacerate Hits, and Allows you to stack momentum faster at T2+.
Aspect of the Agile Blade
Gives + Aerial Ace Duration. Good QOL.
Aspect of the Unstoppable Force
Gives + Aerial Ace Damage. Decent Damage Increase.
Aspect of the Stellar Fury
Increases Multihit hits, very good for DPS.
Aspect of the Pinwheel
Increases Lacerate range, Allowing for a easier time hitting enemies.
